The Art of Touch

Haptic learning applies the same principles to physical comfort. By correlating different types of touch with a user's bio-feedback (like slower breathing or relaxed muscles), the robot learns how to provide the right comforting gesture at the right time.

A bubble chart can represent different touch types, where bubble size corresponds to the user's stress level it is suited for.
